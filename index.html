About

I am a machine learning engineer in Greater Seattle. I work on recommendation systems, ranking and retrieval, transformer models, sequential modeling, and production ML infrastructure.

My recent work spans subscriber lifecycle modeling at Disney Streaming, personalized behavioral-health systems at SonderMind and Mindstrong, and healthcare machine learning research at Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center and UC San Diego Health.

Experience

  • Lead Machine Learning Engineer, Disney Streaming, The Walt Disney Company. Transformer-based subscriber lifecycle intelligence, churn forecasting, intervention timing, Spark/Databricks pipelines, MLflow, Unity Catalog, AWS, and production prediction systems.
  • Senior Applied Scientist, Tech Lead, SonderMind. Recommendation systems, semantic retrieval, neural ranking, transformer sequence models, scheduling optimization, and personalized behavioral-health experiences.
  • Senior Applied Scientist, Mindstrong. Personalized behavioral-health ML systems, adaptive recommendations, measurement-based care workflows, and production infrastructure with Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, and CI/CD.
  • Research Data Scientist, Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center. Statistical modeling and machine learning for longitudinal multimodal healthcare data, clinical datasets, wearable-device data, and EHR workflows.
  • Research Engineer, UC San Diego Health. Machine learning pipelines and large-scale data-processing workflows for medical imaging and clinical datasets.
