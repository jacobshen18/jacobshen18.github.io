Profile

I build AI and machine learning systems that connect user behavior, product context, model inference, and large-scale data infrastructure. At Disney Streaming, I work on transformer-based subscriber lifecycle intelligence, including churn forecasting, intervention timing, batch inference, production prediction publishing, and monitoring.

Before Disney, I worked in behavioral health technology at SonderMind and Mindstrong, where I developed real-time prediction, recommendation, retrieval, ranking, and sequence modeling systems for personalized digital experiences. Earlier, I worked in healthcare research settings on statistical modeling and machine learning for clinical, wearable-device, imaging, and longitudinal health datasets.
